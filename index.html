MaratonIME Winter School

The MaratonIME ICPC Winter School 2017 is organized by MaratonIME and hosted by IME-USP during two weeks from July 10th to July 21st. Our main goal is to train ICPC contestants from all over the world for the national regionals and the World Finals.

There will be two former World Finalists as instructors, one for each week. We expect to receive students from other countries, specially from Texas A&M. Also, one instructor is from Slovakia so all the activities will be held in English.

All interested students must register individually through the registration page. If you have a team, all three members must register individually. There is no fee, but we will not provide any kind of food, housing or transportation.

We will live stream all the lectures on YouTube. You can take part of the contests remotely. Don't forget to like our facebook page for further information and photos.
